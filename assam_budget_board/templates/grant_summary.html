Total Grant Wise Budget Allocation
The bubble chart provides an overview of grant-wise allocations made by the Government of Assam in financial year 2019-20. The bubbles also depict the percentage changes in allocations as compared to the budget estimates of 2018-19.

Colour of the bubbles represent increases / cuts in allocations in 2019-20 as compared the budget estimates of 2018-19.

  • –25%
  • –10%
  • 0
  • +10%
  • +25%

The size of the bubble represents the amount of allocations relative to other departments.
